## Account Setup

Creating an account on HeyFounder.in is straightforward and takes just a few minutes. Whether you're a founder looking to list your startup or an investor seeking opportunities, our registration process ensures you get started quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit the homepage and click 'Sign Up'. Provide your email, name, and choose your role (Founder or Investor).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="settings">
    Add profile details, including location and interests, to personalize your experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore" icon="search">
    Start browsing startups or list your own business.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">Ensure your email is valid for account verification and notifications.</Callout>

## Listing Your Startup

As a founder, listing your startup is easy and increases your visibility to potential investors.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Log in and navigate to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Startup" icon="plus-circle">
    Click 'Add Startup' and fill in details like name, description, category, and location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Assets" icon="image">
    Add logo, screenshots, and any relevant documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="send">
    Review and publish your listing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">Your startup will appear in relevant searches and featured lists once published.</Callout>

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What information is required to list a startup?" default-open="false">
    You'll need to provide the startup name, description, category, location, team size, and current funding stage.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Can I edit my listing after publishing?" default-open="false">
    Yes, you can edit your listing at any time through your dashboard.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Connecting with Others

HeyFounder.in facilitates connections between founders and investors through direct messaging and contact forms.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Founders" icon="message-square">
    Respond to investor inquiries and share more details about your business.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Investors" icon="users">
    Reach out to founders with your interest and investment proposals.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<CodeGroup tabs="Python,JavaScript">
  ````python
  # Send a connection request
  import requests
  data = {'startup_id': 123, 'message': 'Interested in investing'}
  response = requests.post('/api/connections', json=data)
  ````
  ````javascript
  // Send connection request
  const data = { startup_id: 123, message: 'Interested in investing' };
  const response = await fetch('/api/connections', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: { 'Content-Type': 'application/json' },
    body: JSON.stringify(data)
  });
  ````
</CodeGroup>

<Callout kind="alert">Always verify connections and conduct due diligence before sharing sensitive information.</Callout>
